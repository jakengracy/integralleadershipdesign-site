Change Management &#8211; Integral Leadership Design

January 1, 2019

Future-Ready Your Organization By Driving Success Through Change Management

In the rapidly evolving business landscape, the organization of the future is unfolding now. Driving success through change management focuses on the human aspect of organizational transformations. It’s not only about addressing issues, adapting to market trends, or leveraging new opportunities—it’s about instilling a new direction across the organization, cultivating different work methods, and incorporating new technology.

Engage Your People

In our contemporary world, change is a constant. Successful navigation through these changes necessitates the engagement of the entire workforce, beyond just a select few leaders. Hence, attention must be given to:

Establishing a change strategy developed collaboratively Allowing employees to take ownership of implementation plans Adopting a communication approach centered on dialogue rather than instruction

By adopting these approaches, organizations can ensure that their employees remain active participants throughout the change process, thus leading to improved, accelerated, and enduring results. Additionally, encouraging employees to participate in the design of organizational changes, a process known as co-creation, enables organizations to better identify and meet employee needs.

Build Commitment To Change

Co-creation fosters stronger relationships among stakeholders as it encourages active participation. It also builds greater employee loyalty towards the organization. This is not only because co-creation encourages positive interaction but also because it results in a work environment that better aligns with the needs and expectations of employees.

Optimize Your Success

Achieving success in any project requires not only a well-crafted plan but also effective mobilization of your organization’s human element. This includes your team, culture, core values, and behavioural patterns. Today’s organizations must master leading and managing change.

We optimize your change process by focusing on

Purpose: Tie every change to a compelling, shared goal grounded in strategic thinking. Shared Strategy: Engage in the driving and delivery of crucial changes. Leadership: Foster and align leadership with change. Critical Mass: Involve people directly in the process. Roadmap: Co-create a visible journey of change. Infrastructure: Support the creation of a structure for leading, managing, and sustaining change. Momentum: Connect change initiatives to your organization’s culture and values.

Transformations often fail, not from incorrect solutions, but from the struggle to optimize the change process for successful execution. Therefore, organizations that put their people at the heart of the change process consistently achieve their desired outcomes.

We ensure your change management efforts stay people-focused and move in harmony towards your goals. This is achieved this by using Design Thinking and Agile Change Management, keeping your change efforts human-centred and cohesive.

What results and impact can you expect?

 We design your change process so:

The optimal design solution gets created

Stakeholders are engaged early so they commit rather than resist the change

Implementation runs smoothly, including any needed course corrections

Required shifts in mindset, behaviour and culture occur as part of the change

The design solution is adopted and sustained by all stakeholder groups

Intended ROI is achieved, on time and on budget

Change capability throughout is developed as part of the process

Certified Change Management Professional™ (CCMP™)

We are proud to have earned the CCMP™ which is a globally recognized credential established by the Association of Change Management Professionals (ACMP) for professionals to demonstrate their commitment to leading the way change works.

The CCMP™ was developed based on ACMP’s industry-leading Standard for Change Management© that defines best practices in change management. The CCMP™ is a way for professionals to demonstrate their investment in ongoing development while successfully applying their experience to respond to a breadth of change management situations.
