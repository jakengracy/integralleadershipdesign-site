Not every leadership challenge requires a months-long engagement. Sometimes you need clarity on one specific situation — a difficult conversation you've been avoiding, a career decision with real consequences, a team dynamic that's gotten away from you, or a moment of transition that needs a clear head and an honest outside perspective.

That's what a Single Strategy Session with Alexandra is for.

## What It Is

A focused, 90-minute one-on-one session conducted via video conference. You bring the situation. Alexandra brings twenty-five years of leadership coaching experience, deep listening, and the ability to ask the questions that cut through the noise.

The session is structured to move quickly from context to clarity — so you leave with real perspective, not just validation.

## What You Might Bring

Single sessions work well for:

- A high-stakes decision you're stuck on
- A difficult conversation you need to prepare for
- A leadership challenge you're navigating without a trusted sounding board
- A career inflection point — a promotion, a transition, a role change
- A team or relationship dynamic that's affecting your effectiveness
- A moment of doubt about your direction or approach

## What Happens in the Session

Alexandra will listen carefully, ask questions that surface what's really going on, and work with you to identify the clearest path forward. There's no predetermined agenda — the session goes where you need it to go.

You won't leave with a generic action plan. You'll leave with a perspective that's genuinely yours.

## Is This the Right Starting Point?

A Single Strategy Session is also a natural way to explore whether a longer coaching engagement might be right for you — without committing to one upfront. Many clients who begin with a single session go on to work with Alexandra over a longer arc, once they've experienced what the work can produce.

If you're uncertain whether coaching is for you, this is the lowest-risk way to find out.
