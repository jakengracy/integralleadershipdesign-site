Leadership doesn't develop in a vacuum. It develops through reflection, challenge, and the kind of honest conversation that's hard to have inside your organization. That's what individual coaching provides — a dedicated space to work on yourself as a leader, with someone who has no agenda other than your growth.

Eighty percent of Fortune 500 companies now offer executive coaching to their leaders. The reason is straightforward: when a leader improves, their entire team and organization benefits.

## What Individual Coaching Looks Like

One-on-one coaching with Alexandra is a sustained engagement — not a series of disconnected conversations, but a progressive journey with a clear arc. Sessions are conducted via video conference, making it accessible regardless of where you're based.

Typical engagements run 6–12 months, with sessions every two to three weeks. The work happens between sessions as much as during them.

## The Process

Every coaching engagement begins with a thorough assessment — exploring your aspirations, your current context, the challenges you're navigating, and the habits or mindsets that may be standing in your way.

From there, each session combines deep listening, open-ended questions, and honest challenge. Alexandra brings a direct style with a light touch — the kind that helps you see hard truths without making them feel insurmountable.

## What You Can Expect to Work On

Individual coaching engagements are highly personalized, but common themes include:

- Stepping into a new leadership role with confidence
- Managing difficult relationships or team dynamics
- Strengthening your executive presence and communication
- Navigating organizational change or uncertainty
- Clarifying your leadership identity and direction
- Balancing ambition with sustainability

## Who This Is For

Individual coaching is for aspiring and current executives and managers who recognize that their own growth is the highest-leverage investment they can make — in their teams, their organizations, and their own careers.

If you're someone who has the drive but wants the guidance to unlock your full potential, this is for you.

## Results You Can Expect

Clients consistently report greater clarity on who they are as a leader, improved relationships with their teams and stakeholders, increased confidence in high-stakes situations, and a stronger sense of purpose and direction in their work.

The work is deeply personal. The results show up everywhere.
