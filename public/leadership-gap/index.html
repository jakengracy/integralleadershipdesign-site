The Leadership Gap: Rethinking who you are

January 1, 2019

It’s pop quiz time. Match the leadership archetypes with leaders who embody them.

Here are your archetype choices:

The Rebel | The Explorer | The Truth Teller | The Hero | The Navigator | The Knight | The Inventor

Here are your leader choices:

Sheryl Sandberg

Winston Churchil

Walt Disney

Malala Yousafzai

Mother Teresa

Got it figured out? Excellent. Spend a few moments thinking about which of those leaders most resembles your leadership style. Ask yourself, do others experience you as you see yourself? Are you flexible in your leadership style?

Want to learn more? In Lolly Daskal’s book, The Leadership Gap, she introduces us to a system of seven archetypes that will help you view yourself objectively so that you can identify the gaps you face as you work towards greatness.
