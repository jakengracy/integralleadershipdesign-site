Our Approach

Enquire. Engage. Innovate. Impact.

Real change doesn't come from a template. It comes from understanding what's actually happening — in an organization, in a team, in a leader — and building from there.

Alexandra works from a whole-person, whole-system perspective. That means looking beneath the surface, asking the harder questions, and designing solutions that fit the specific context of each client rather than borrowing from someone else's playbook.

About Alexandra

Twenty Years at the Intersection of Leadership and Change

Alexandra Salamis is a certified leadership coach and change management consultant — and a rare combination of both. She has spent two decades working with executives, senior leaders, and organizations across Canada, bringing deep corporate experience, rigorous coaching methodology, and genuine investment in the people she works with.

She is a member of the Forbes Coaches Council, holds the Certified Change Management Professional (CCMP) designation, and has been nominated for the Governor General's Award in Gender Equality for her lifelong work developing women in leadership.

Her clients describe her as perceptive, direct, and honest — someone who can help you see hard truths without making them feel insurmountable.
