About Alexandra

Award-Winning Leadership Coach & Change Consultant

Alexandra Salamis is the founder of Integral Leadership Design — an award-winning leadership development and change consultant with over twenty years of experience empowering aspiring and current leaders around the world.

Nominated for Canada's Top 100 Most Powerful Women Award in 2018, Alex has been named one of the top 21 coaches in Ottawa by Influence Digest Magazine and recognized as a Top Leadership Coach by Coach Foundation.

She has dedicated herself to coaching organizations and individuals in sustainable change, ensuring the next generation of leaders is prepared to lead with conviction, empathy, and courage.
