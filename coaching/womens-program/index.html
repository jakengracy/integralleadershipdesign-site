Women's Leadership Program

Purpose, Passion, and Presence for Women Rising — Alexandra Salamis's signature coaching program for women stepping into authentic, confident leadership.

Women bring enormous leadership potential to their organizations. Too often, that potential goes underdeveloped — not for lack of ability, but because the environments, expectations, and support systems women navigate are genuinely different from those of their male peers.

Alexandra Salamis has spent her career working at that gap. Her signature program — Purpose, Passion, and Presence for Women Rising — was designed specifically for women who are ready to lead with confidence, authenticity, and real impact.

What the Program Addresses

The program works across five interconnected pillars, each targeting a real challenge that women in leadership consistently face:

Presence — How you show up in a room, own your space, and command attention without performing a version of leadership that doesn’t fit you.

Voice — How you communicate with authority, assert your perspective, and navigate the dynamics that can silence capable women in organizational settings.

Organizational Navigation — How to read and move through the political and cultural landscape of your organization effectively, without compromising your integrity.

Resilience — How to manage setbacks, self-doubt, and the particular pressures women leaders absorb — and come back stronger.

Values Alignment — How to lead from a place that’s genuinely yours, so your leadership is sustainable and feels right, not just effective.

Who This Program Is For

This program is for women who:

  • Are in leadership or moving toward it, and want to show up at their full potential
  • Have been told they’re capable but feel something is holding them back
  • Want to lead more authentically — not to fit a mold, but to define their own
  • Are navigating a significant transition: a new role, a promotion, a pivot
  • Want to build the kind of confidence that comes from the inside, not just from external validation

What Participants Say

Participants consistently describe this program as more than professional development — it’s a turning point. Many report that it clarified not just how they lead, but who they are as leaders.

“I am more authentic and way more self-confident. My path forward is clear. It has been a life changer for me.”

“Alexandra’s dedication and passion are definitively contagious. I feel not only empowered to be more authentic and lead with my best self, but also supported by a network of empowered women she champions.”

Format

The program is delivered via video conference and designed to be accessible regardless of your location or schedule. Alexandra works with participants individually and, where applicable, in small cohort settings that build peer connection alongside personal growth.
